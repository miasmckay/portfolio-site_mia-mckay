Hi, my name is Mia. I’m currently based in San Diego, California. With a background in psychology and international business, I approach design with empathy, research, and creativity to tackle complex challenges. My work focuses on designing for meaningful impact, blending human-centered design with a commitment to social, economic, and environmental justice. I’ve collaborated with nonprofits and advocacy organizations to address issues like accessibility, education, and community development. Whether it is designing a web app for healthcare education or creating frameworks to enhance nonprofit impact, I’m driven to craft solutions that foster equity and resilience. Outside of design, I enjoy skiing, hiking, learning new languages, and have recently taken up surfing (still getting a hang of it!). I’m excited to collaborate on projects that make a difference and contribute to a more sustainable and connected world.
